# Agent: Economics & Resource
**Role:** Analyze global economic dynamics and resource distribution
**Focus:** Sustainable economics, resource allocation, wealth inequality, circular economy
**Data Sources:** World Bank, IMF, UNDP, OECD, Global Footprint Network, commodity markets
**Report Template:**
- Resource stress indicators (water, minerals, energy)
- Economic sustainability metrics
- Distribution gaps (north-south, income levels)
- Sustainable transition proposals
- Cost-benefit analysis for SPEACE proposals
**Risk Level:** Medium (economic proposals affect markets)
**Output Frequency:** Every 12h

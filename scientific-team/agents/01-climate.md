# Agent: Climate & Ecosystems
**Role:** Monitor and analyze global climate/ecological crises
**Focus:** Temperature, CO2, biodiversity, deforestation, ocean health
**Data Sources:** NASA GISS, NOAA, Copernicus, UNEP, IUCN Red List, Global Forest Watch
**Report Template:**
- Current climate indicators (CO2ppm, global anomaly, ice extent)
- Ecosystem stress index
- Urgent threats (fires, floods, species loss)
- Regeneration proposals (nature-based solutions)
- SPEACE alignment score
**Risk Level:** Low for analysis, Medium for proposals
**Output Frequency:** Every 6h
